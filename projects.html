John F Kennedy International Airport, New York, Terminal 4

The Team of LCOR/Schiphol-USA has been selected by the Port Authority of New York and New Jersey to provide the first privately funded Terminal Redevelopment at JFK in exchange for a 20-year lease on the new facility.

The International Arrivals Building - IAB was the first terminal constructed at John F. Kennedy International Airport (at that time Idlewild Airport). It serves as the US gateway to the majority of foreign flag carriers. The east and west wings of the facility support the departure operations.
